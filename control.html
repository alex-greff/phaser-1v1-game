Tip for
yellow asteroids
Player 1 has the capability to
stop the asteroid by shooting
an orb at it with the button f
and then firing it at your
opponent by shooting it with a
laser with the button g

It is important to remember
you can only do this with the
yellow asteroids and no other
Player 2 has the capability to
stop the asteroid by shooting
an orb at it with the left arrow key
and then firing it at your
opponent by shooting it with a
laser with the down arrow key

It is important to remember
you can only do this with the
yellow asteroids and no other
