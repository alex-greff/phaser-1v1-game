StoryLine

Once a long time ago something was stolen from the Goswaminners and the Jeffs.
It has been too long to remember who stole what but for their losses,
the Goswaminners and the Jeffs blamed each other.
A mighty battle raged for centuries until both sides had lost a great deal of people and their homes
so much that they decided to end it once and for all!
They decided to send two of their best pilots (one from each side)
to battle it out in space near the asteriods and the winner will be awarded
while the losing team is destroyed!

Start Page

The start Page has many buttons and functions.
If you do not already know ; the start button starts the game,
the controls button tell you the controls, and the info button gives you
information about the game (backstory etc..). The customization option
allows you to customize your game for a different expirience. Just click the
add and subtract buttons to change the amount of ammo, and the health.
